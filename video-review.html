Editing Review Rubric

Category Good (1) Great (2) Excellent (3) Outstanding (4)
🎬 Narrative Flow & Storytelling Basic flow; disjointed cuts and unclear storytelling. Clear narrative with moderate pacing and occasional creative elements. Well-paced sequence with engaging storytelling and creative transitions. Exceptionally engaging narrative; expert-level pacing and flow.
✂️ Cutting & Transitions Basic cuts with occasional jarring transitions. Mostly smooth editing with some creative transitions. Seamless cuts and well-integrated transitions that enhance the flow. Flawless editing with innovative, perfectly timed transitions.

✨ Visual Effects & Graphics Basic graphics and minimal effects. Effective graphics with occasional creative effects. High-quality effects and graphics that enhance content. Cinematic-quality visuals and innovative effects that elevate the edit.
🎨 Color Grading & Visual Consistency Basic correction; inconsistent visual tone. Consistent grading with a generally appealing visual tone. Professional color grading that enhances mood and cohesion. Masterful grading with a striking, cohesive aesthetic.
🖥️ Technical Quality & Export Acceptable quality with minor technical issues. Good resolution and rendering quality. High-quality export that meets industry standards. Flawless technical execution with perfect optimization.
🌟 Overall Impact Meets basic requirements; limited impact. Enhances the viewer experience with solid editing. Highly engaging and professional editing. Extraordinary editing that elevates the entire production.

Legend & Definitions

Narrative Flow & Storytelling: Evaluate the overall narrative, pacing, and engagement.

Cutting & Transitions: Assess the smoothness, timing, and creativity of cuts and transitions.

Audio Quality & Synchronization: Measure clarity, mix quality, and sync with visuals.

Visual Effects & Graphics: Consider the quality and appropriateness of effects and graphics.

Color Grading & Visual Consistency: Review the color correction and overall visual tone.

Technical Quality & Export: Check resolution, export quality, and technical polish.

Overall Impact: Summarize how effectively the editing enhances the viewer’s experience.

Rating Levels
  • Good (1): Meets the basics but lacks strong impact. Action: Refine pacing, transitions, and clarity.
  • Great (2): Solid execution with minor areas for improvement. Action: Enhance creative elements and polish.
  • Excellent (3): Professional and engaging editing; room for slight refinement. Action: Push creative boundaries.
  • Outstanding (4): Extraordinary editing that elevates the production. Action: Maintain or exceed this standard.
