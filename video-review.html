Editing Review Rubric

Category Good (1) Great (2) Excellent (3) Outstanding (4)
🌟 Overall Impact Meets basic requirements but lacks significant emotional or creative impact. An engaging edit that supports the video’s purpose with noticeable creative effort. Highly effective editing that engages the viewer and enhances the content’s impact. Masterful editing that captivates the audience, evokes strong emotions, and drives action.
🎬 Narrative Flow & Storytelling The video has a general structure but suffers from frequent logical gaps or abrupt scene changes. The narrative follows a recognizable structure with occasional lapses in clarity. The narrative is engaging, with clear sequencing and transitions that support the story. Transitions are smooth and effectively enhance pacing and continuity. Cuts are executed with precision, employing creative transitions that elevate the edit.

Good resolution and export quality with only minor technical imperfections. High-quality export meeting industry standards, with correct aspect ratio and no visible artifacts. Exceptional technical quality with pristine resolution, flawless export, and consistent frame rate.

Content is compelling and resonates well with the target audience. Exceptionally engaging content that deeply resonates and inspires the audience.

Thoughtfully integrated sound design with well-chosen music and balanced effects. Exemplary use of music and sound design that elevates the emotional and narrative impact.

Effective use of text, lower-thirds, and simple overlays that support the content. High-quality effects and graphics enhance the storytelling without distraction. Well-designed on-screen text and titles enhance understanding without distraction. Exemplary use of text and titles that are stylistically consistent, clear, and powerfully support the message.

Legend & Definitions

Overall Impact: Measures viewer engagement, emotional resonance, creative flair, and call-to-action strength.

Narrative Flow & Storytelling: Evaluates the overall structure and clarity of the story.

Cutting & Transitions: Assesses how seamlessly cuts and transitions support the narrative.

Pacing & Timing: Measures the tempo and rhythm of the edit, ensuring scenes maintain engagement.

Technical Quality & Export: Evaluates resolution, aspect ratio, absence of compression artifacts, and frame rate consistency.

Story/Content Engagement: Assesses how compelling and resonant the content is with its target audience.

Use of Music & Sound Design: Reviews how background music and sound effects enhance the edit.

Visual Effects & Graphics: Considers the use of text, lower-thirds, overlays, and overall graphic polish.

Motion Graphics / Animated Elements: Assesses the creativity and effectiveness of animated elements.

On-Screen Text & Titles: Evaluates clarity, style, and how well on-screen text supports the message.

Rating Levels
  • Good (1): Meets basic requirements but with noticeable shortcomings.
  • Great (2): Solid execution with minor areas for improvement.
  • Excellent (3): Professional quality with only slight refinements needed.
  • Outstanding (4): Exceeds industry standards with exceptional execution.
