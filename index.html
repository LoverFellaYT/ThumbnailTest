Explanation of Each Criterion

  1. Curiosity & Intrigue: Thumbnails that immediately hook viewers with a mysterious or surprising element raise CTR significantly.
  2. Main Subject Clarity: A clearly defined focal point ensures viewers immediately understand the thumbnail‚Äôs subject.
  3. Human Element & Emotion: Including a face or strong emotional reaction can create an instant emotional bond with the viewer.
  4. Game Context Clarity: Making the featured game obvious helps attract fans of that game and provides immediate recognition.
  5. Title-Thumbnail Synergy: The thumbnail should complement the title‚Äôs story or promise, not just repeat it.
  6. Simplicity & Focus: Thumbnails with minimal clutter and a strong focus look better at small sizes and stand out more.
  7. Color & Contrast: Bold, complementary colors and high contrast help thumbnails pop in a crowded feed.
  8. Composition & Layout: Balanced placement of elements ensures the eye naturally goes to the most important part first.
  9. Text Usage (If Any): Keep text short, legible, and used only if it adds intrigue beyond the title.
  10. Authenticity & Accuracy: Misleading thumbnails may yield clicks initially but hurt watch time and trust long-term.
  11. Technical Quality: Clear, high-resolution images convey professionalism and draw more clicks.
