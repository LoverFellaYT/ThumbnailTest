Legend & Definitions

Scroll‑Stopping Power: Focuses on the pure visual impact—bold colors, high contrast, dynamic layouts, and striking imagery that immediately grab attention. Note: Color contrast here is one tool among several visual techniques.

Composition & Design: Evaluates the overall balance, spatial organization, and adherence to design principles such as the rule‑of‑thirds.

Visual Hierarchy & Clarity: Measures how effectively the design guides the viewer’s eye through appropriate scaling and positioning of elements.

Text Clarity: Assesses legibility, conciseness, and accessibility (sufficient contrast and clear fonts) of overlaid text.

Emotional & Curiosity Hook: Focuses on the psychological pull—the ability of the thumbnail’s imagery or text to provoke an emotional response or spark intrigue.

Thumbnail–Title Synergy: Looks at how well the thumbnail and title work together to create a cohesive message without redundancy.

Thumbnail–Video Accuracy: Ensures that the thumbnail accurately represents the video content, building trust with the viewer.

Channel Identity & Branding: Evaluates consistency in brand elements (logos, fonts, color schemes) that distinguish the channel from others. This is separate from general color usage which is about aesthetic harmony.

Technical Polish: Considers image resolution, aspect ratio, and overall clarity, ensuring that technical standards (e.g. 1280×720, 16:9) are met.

Color Usage: Reviews the overall color palette for harmony and brand alignment, independent of the dynamic visual impact assessed in Scroll‑Stopping Power.

Rating Levels
  • Good (1): Meets the bare minimum requirements; design and impact are weak. Action: Increase contrast, balance, and refine text.
  • Great (2): Contains some impactful elements but still lacks cohesion. Action: Enhance visual balance and brand alignment.
  • Excellent (3): Highly effective design with clear focal points and readable text. Action: Refine for a more unique impact.
  • Outstanding (4): Practically flawless design that is both visually arresting and emotionally compelling. Action: Continue experimenting with advanced design techniques while maintaining brand consistency.
