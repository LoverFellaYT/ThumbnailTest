Legend & Definitions

Scroll‑Stopping Power: Use bold colors, dynamic movement, or expressive faces. High scores correlate with high CTR.

Composition & Design: Achieve balance using the rule‑of‑thirds, clear visual flow, and strategic negative space. Consistent color harmony is key.

Visual Hierarchy & Clarity: Guide the viewer’s eye by scaling elements appropriately—largest for the key subject, then supporting text or imagery.

Text Clarity: Keep text concise, legible on mobile, and impactful. Use power words and limit text length.

Curiosity Gap: Generate intrigue by posing a question or hinting at an outcome without revealing too much.

Emotional Hook or Reaction: Use humor, shock, nostalgia, or empathy to create a strong emotional connection.

Thumbnail–Title Synergy: Ensure the thumbnail and title complement each other without repeating the same information.

Thumbnail–Video Accuracy: The thumbnail should accurately represent the video’s content—avoid misleading imagery.

Channel Identity & Branding: Reflect consistent brand elements (colors, logos, style) to build recognition.

Technical Polish: Use high‑resolution images (at least 1280×720, 16:9 aspect ratio), optimize file size (under 2 MB), and test on multiple devices.

Rating Levels
  • Good (1): Meets the bare essentials but lacks impact. Action: Improve contrast, add a focal point, and reduce clutter.
  • Great (2): Contains some impactful elements, but minor distractions remain. Action: Remove competing elements and refine the text.
  • Excellent (3): Highly effective with clear focal points; could push further on uniqueness. Action: Try adding a surprising visual cue or punchier text.
  • Outstanding (4): Practically flawless and compelling at a glance. Action: Experiment with advanced design techniques while staying on-brand.
