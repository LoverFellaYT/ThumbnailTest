How to Use Replay mod & FAQ

How to Use

  • Click on each rating cell to score the replay shot.
  • The Overall Score (out of 28) updates live as you select ratings.
  • Focus on objective criteria such as composition, movement, timing, lighting, technical quality, and creativity.
  • Provide your comments in the Reviewer Feedback section.
  • Use the Self-Assessment Checklist to reflect on your shot’s quality.
  • Reset scores or export your review as a PNG when finished.

Tips & Tricks

  • Ensure your shot follows classic cinematography principles (e.g. rule of thirds, balanced composition).
  • Smooth camera movements add a professional touch—practice panning and tracking.
  • Good lighting and color grading set the mood; adjust your settings accordingly.
  • Experiment with creative angles to add visual interest.
  • Check your technical quality; a clear, stable shot is key.

FAQ

  • Q: How do I improve my composition?
    A: Study visual balance, use guidelines like the rule of thirds, and experiment with framing.
  • Q: What makes camera movement effective?
    A: Consistency, smoothness, and purposeful motion that supports the narrative.
  • Q: How can I ensure proper lighting?
    A: Adjust your light sources and use color grading tools to create the desired atmosphere.
  • Q: What technical aspects should I focus on?
    A: Resolution, focus, and stability are crucial for a clear and professional shot.
